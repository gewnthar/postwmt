Instructions & Data Access Info

Instructions

  • Copy and paste the scheduler text from the "My Schedule" page/section in WMT into the main text area above.
  • Click the desired button: "Submit to Google Calendar" (if logged in) or "Generate ICS File".
  • Note on Updates: Clicking "Submit to Google Calendar" will first remove any previously added shifts (created by this tool and tagged #postwmt) within the date range of your pasted schedule before adding the new ones. This allows you to easily update a changed schedule.
  • Formats Understood:
    • Start hours (0-23) for 8-hour shifts (e.g., `8`).
    • `TEN` for 10-hour shifts (e.g., `8TEN`).
    • `$` for overtime shifts (e.g., `8$` or `8TEN$`).
    • `X` for Regular Days Off (RDO - these are ignored).
    • `A<HH>` for Annual Leave (creates an 8-hour event starting at hour HH, e.g., `A<13>` for 1 PM).
    • `AOA<HH>` for "At Or After" OT (creates an 8hr shift starting at HH, plus a 2hr OT event immediately after).
    • `AOB<HH>` for "At Or Before" OT (creates a 2hr OT event immediately before an 8hr shift that starts at HH).
    • `T<HH>` for Training Shifts (creates an 8hr event starting at HH).
  • DST Note: Shifts starting/ending near Daylight Saving Time changes may need manual verification.
